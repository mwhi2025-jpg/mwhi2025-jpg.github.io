Tucson Comic and Toy Expo

On October 11th there will be a comic and toy expo. A wide variety of comics, toys, and collectable venders will be selling their merchandise for affordable prices. There will be food and drink for sale by the venue

The venue will be open from 9am to 3pm located on North Stone Avenue in Tucson Arizona. Admission is $5 per person and parking is free.
